Privacy Policy for 156 Driver Manager App

 

At 156 Driver Manager, we are committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy applies to our Driver Manager app and explains how we collect, use, and disclose information through the app.

 

Information Collection and Use

 

The 156 Driver Manager app only collects the location data of its users. This information is used to provide accurate and up-to-date information about the user's location, allowing the app to provide more efficient and personalized services.

 

Location Data

 

The 156 Driver Manager app uses GPS and other location-based technologies to determine the location of its users. Location data is collected only when the app is in use and can be turned off at any time by disabling location services in the device's settings.

 

Disclosure of Information

 

156 Driver Manager does not sell, rent, or share any personal information, including location data, with third parties for marketing purposes. We may disclose information, including location data, to third-party service providers as necessary to provide the services offered through the 156 Driver Manager app.

 

Security

 

156 Driver Manager takes the security of its users' information, including location data, seriously. We have implemented appropriate physical, electronic, and administrative safeguards to protect the information we collect and use through the 156 Driver Manager app.

 

Changes to This Privacy Policy

 

156 Driver Manager may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or to comply with applicable laws. We encourage our users to regularly review this Privacy Policy to stay informed about our privacy practices.
